Game Jams are like improv contests: You don't know the theme until they start, and you have limited time to make something.  
It's a competition with lightweight stakes and lightweight prizes - but you ultimately win by trying.

## The Theme: "Only One"


This was a fun project with two goals:
1. Learn how to make a game.
2. Finish and publish the game.
